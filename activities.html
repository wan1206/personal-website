My Activities

Throughout my time at university, I have participated in various activities that have helped build my leadership and teamwork skills. Among them are:


-Participating in university events such as fun runs, Solidarity Week, and others.


-Serving as a media committee member for the OBS Beginner Crash Course programme organized by PSK Club members.


-Involving myself in community service by helping the elderly at the Ketoyong senior care centre and assisting in sorting clothes to be donated to underprivileged families and flood victims.


These activities have not only provided me with valuable experiences but have also taught me the importance of communication and collaboration.
